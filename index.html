Our Vision & University

--University Vision Statement 2025

History

Established in 1975, Bharatiya Vidyapeeth University has been a pioneer in providing quality education for over 50 years. Our institution has consistently ranked amoung the Top 10 Universities in India.

Read Complete History

The university was founded by Dr. Patangrao Kadam with a vision to provide affordable and quality education to all sections of society. Starting with just 50 students, we now have 0ver 25,000 students from 50+ countries.

Major milestones include receiving UGC recognition in 1980, achieving deemed university status in 1996, and receiving 'A+' grade from NAAC in 2020.
